Opusmodus                                                                   log-of-gamma   1

log-of-gamma x
[Function]

Arguments and Values:
x                        a sequence of numbers.


Description:

Given x (a real or complex valued number whose real part is greater than 0), returns the log
of gamma x.

(log-of-gamma '(1 4 3 7 5 8))
=> (3.083838695516761D-8 1.791759411082495D0 0.6931473006748217D0
    6.579251066941406D0 3.1780533816915506D0 8.525161082700862D0)
