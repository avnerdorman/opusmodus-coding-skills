Description:

OPUSMODUS carries a rich and diverse list of chord abbreviations. These can be employed
in all kinds of situations, as the examples below demonstrate. The last example is particularly
powerful, showing how a complex chord such as a diminished chord with a major 7th
can be mapped to a chromatic scale and result in unique scale.
