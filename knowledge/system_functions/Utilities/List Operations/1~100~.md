This utility function is invaluable for calling a sublist or section from within a list of lists.

(setf list '((c4 d4 e4 f4 g4) (a4 b4))

(1~ list)
=> (c4 d4 e4 f4 g4)

(2~ list)
=> (a4 b4)

In the preliminary example below a randomly generator list of integers has split up into motif
groups. The groups have then been re-arranged by collecting from odd and even counts of
the divided list.

    (4 6 8 9) (3 3 4 4) (2 5) (4))

(setf odd-l (append (1~ lis-i) (3~ lis-i) (5~ lis-i)))
=> (2 2 3 5 10 4 11 3 3 4 4)
